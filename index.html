Marma

Regenerative molecules, engineered with intent

Marma is building a platform for regenerative therapeutics — molecules designed to repair, restore, and reconnect biological systems at the level where damage actually occurs. Our focus is on translatable science: interventions that move from bench to clinic with rigor and clarity.
